South Eastern Kenya University "SEKU" is a public university with its main campus located in Kwa Vonza, Kitui County, Kenya.

Kitui Town Campus is one of the satellite campuses of South Eastern Kenya University, situated in Kitui Town, within the Manyenyoni-BLI area, about 600 m off the Kitui-Mutomo road.

SEKU envisions becoming a World Class University with quality teaching, creative learning, innovative research and strong community outreach.

The University College was granted Charter on 1st March 2013 to become the South Eastern Kenya University (SEKU). Further, the University is governed by the Universities Act of 2012, University Charter and Statutes.

South Eastern Kenya University ranked 18th in Kenya, 7073rd in the global 2023 rating, and scored in 38 research topics.
